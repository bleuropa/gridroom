# Deferred Decisions

## Overview

This document tracks product decisions that have been intentionally deferred. These are not forgotten - they're parked until prerequisites are met or priorities change.

---

## How to Use This Document

- **Add items** when you decide NOT to decide something yet
- **Include clear prerequisites** - what needs to happen first
- **Review regularly** - prerequisites may be met without noticing
- **Remove items** when they're either decided or no longer relevant
