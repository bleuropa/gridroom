# Product Learning Journal

## Overview

This is a living document tracking the evolution of product thinking. Unlike other evergreen docs, this one intentionally includes temporal context to preserve the history of decisions.

---

## How to Use This Document

- **Add entries** when you learn something significant about users, market, or product
- **Include dates** so you can trace the evolution of thinking
- **Be honest** about what didn't work and why
- **Link to evidence** (user feedback, metrics, experiments)
